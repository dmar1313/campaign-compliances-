SMS Messaging Compliance

Program Description

This messaging program sends informational SMS notifications related to property services, inspections, and scheduling. Messages are sent to recipients who have been identified as potentially eligible for services based on publicly available property and location data.

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary. Recipients typically receive one initial outreach message.

Opt-Out Instructions

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP or NO at any time. You will receive no further messages after opting out.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message.

Privacy

We do not share, sell, or distribute your mobile number or personal information to third parties. Your information is used solely to facilitate the messaging program described above.

Carrier Disclaimer

Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
